Digital Transformation

Strategy & Modernisation

Ageing systems can be modernised without disrupting daily operations.

This usually starts with reviewing current platforms, identifying risks, and creating a practical roadmap. The roadmap improves the business in stages rather than replacing everything at once.

The work is broken into manageable phases: stabilise what exists, remove bottlenecks, migrate critical parts, and improve the systems that matter most.

The goal is simple: reduce technical debt, improve reliability, and make technology easier to change as the business grows.

Software Engineering

Web, Mobile & Back-end

Custom software is designed and built for web, mobile, and back-end systems.

Small senior engineering teams handle the work from problem discovery through to production support. They understand the business problem, write the code, and stay close to the outcome.

Modern technology is used where it adds value, but unnecessary complexity is avoided.

The focus is on secure, maintainable software that still makes sense years later. This includes customer portals, internal platforms, APIs, integrations, mobile apps, workflow tools, and systems that replace manual processes.

Data & AI

Platforms & Applied AI

Data and AI solutions should be useful in real business environments, not just impressive in demonstrations.

This can include data warehouses, reporting layers, ETL pipelines, analytics platforms, dashboards, and AI-powered product features.

Practical LLM solutions can also be developed, such as document search, knowledge assistants, RAG systems, and workflow automation.

The focus is on clean data, reliable pipelines, clear governance, and AI features accurate enough to support daily operations.

Managed IT Support

Monitoring & Patching

Managed IT support keeps systems healthy before small problems become business interruptions.

Many IT issues come from avoidable causes: missed patches, failed backups, expired licences, full storage, ageing hardware, or alerts nobody is watching.

Support covers monitoring, patch management, backup checks, user support, licence tracking, and preventative maintenance.

The aim is to reduce downtime, improve security, and give business owners confidence that their IT environment is being actively managed.

Troubleshooting & Diagnostics

Triage & Root-Cause Analysis

Complex IT and software problems often need structured investigation when the cause is not immediately obvious.

This includes recurring outages, slow systems, failed integrations, unclear error messages, unstable applications, network issues, and problems that different suppliers blame on each other.

Structured triage, log analysis, system tracing, infrastructure checks, and root-cause analysis help identify what is really happening.

The goal is not just to apply a quick fix, but to stop the same issue from returning.
